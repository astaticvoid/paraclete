## What happened

The session was consumed by **audio bring-up on the Linux box** rather than
the planned W2 §7.1 exit-criteria pass:

- **Audio "very distorted"** (kick with an audio-rate LFO-like effect,
  confirmed by the user). Root-caused same-day to BUG-012: the device
  delivered 9408-sample callbacks against 512-frame internal blocks, and the
  chunk-and-discard callback dropped ~416 rendered frames per callback —
  desyncing engine state and drifting the clock ~8.8% fast. Server and web
  UI stayed functional; audio output was unusable.
- **Headless start required a `setsid` workaround** — the LaunchpadEmulator
  demanded a TTY even with `--no-tui` (→ INFRA-004).
- **Surface presence assumed at hardcoded IDs** — profiles send LED updates
  unconditionally; no way to ask "is a Launchpad present?" (→ INFRA-005).
- **~4,400 ALSA underruns logged at idle** — no realtime priority on the
  audio thread (→ INFRA-006).
- **Twice, exiting paraclete left pipewire stranded on `auto_null`** —
  system-wide audio loss, KDE showed no output device (→ INFRA-006 impact /
  INFRA-007 workflow fix).
